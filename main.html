The Shawshank Redemption tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a man wrongly convicted of murdering his wife. Sentenced to life in Shawshank prison, Andy faces a brutal and unforgiving environment. Despite the hardship, Andy never loses hope. He befriends fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding and uses his financial skills to navigate the prison's corrupt hierarchy. Over the course of decades, Andy's unwavering hope and quiet determination inspire those around him.

Accolades

The Shawshank Redemption is widely considered one of the greatest films ever made. Despite receiving lukewarm critical reception upon release, it gained a massive cult following and critical acclaim over time. The film holds a 99% rating on Rotten Tomatoes and is consistently ranked at the top of various "best movies" lists.
